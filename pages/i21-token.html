About i21

i21 is designed as the native token of the IRAN NFT ecosystem — a cultural-first experiment on Solana. It aims to connect collectors, artists, and builders around Iranian culture, history, and digital art.

In future phases, i21 will power on-chain governance, marketplace rewards, and curated drops tied to IRAN NFT’s cultural collections.
